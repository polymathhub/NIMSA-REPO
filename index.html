Privacy Policy

At NIMSA-HCTF Medical Portal, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Full name, email, medical school, matric number, and year of study during registration.
  • We update you regularly with NIMSA activities and posts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify student identity and manage registrations.
  • To improve site content and user experience.
  • To send updates and respond to inquiries.

Data Protection

We use secure technologies and Supabase to store your data. Your information is not shared without consent.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your data by contacting hctfnimsa@gmail.com.
