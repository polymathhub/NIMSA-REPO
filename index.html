About NiMSA-HCTF

Our Mission

To strengthen the capacity of medical students through leadership, community service, and impactful health initiatives that foster unity and excellence.

Our Vision

To build a united body of future health professionals committed to innovation, compassion, and service to humanity.

Our Team

A diverse and dynamic community of medical students driven by collaboration, research, and a shared passion for quality healthcare.

Our Impact

Through health outreaches, awareness campaigns, and academic programs, NiMSA-HCTF continues to inspire service and leadership across campuses.

Privacy Policy

At NIMSA-HCTF Medical Portal, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information.

Information We Collect

  • Full name, email, medical school, matric number, and year of study during registration.
  • We update you regularly with NIMSA activities and posts.

How We Use Your Information

  • To verify student identity and manage registrations.
  • To improve site content and user experience.
  • To send updates and respond to inquiries.

Data Protection

We use secure technologies and Supabase to store your data. Your information is not shared without consent.

Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your data by contacting hctfnimsa@gmail.com.
